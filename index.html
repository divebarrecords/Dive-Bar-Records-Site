IN THE ART.

We have made it our mission to give up and coming artists value in their space, offering our trusted network as a vessel for their growth.

Dive Bar Records is the brainchild of Ben O’Shea and Ruairi McLean. Born from the North-West alternative scene.


With a combined two decades experience as artists working our way up, we have an appreciation for the journey small artists embark upon.


In an industry becoming increasingly manufactured, we are pioneering advocates for the Anti-AI, Anti-Corporate movement.


— B. O'Shea & R. McLean
